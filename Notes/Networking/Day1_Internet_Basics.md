# Day 1: Internet Basics

## What is the Internet?

### 1. Nuts-and-Bolts Definition

The Internet is a collection of hardware and software components that work together to connect devices worldwide.

### 2. Networking Infrastructure Definition

The Internet is a networking infrastructure that provides services to distributed applications such as web browsing, email, video streaming, and online gaming.

---

## Protocols

A protocol is a set of rules that defines:

* The format of messages exchanged between communicating entities.
* The order in which messages are sent and received.
* The actions taken when a message is transmitted or received.

**Example:** HTTP, FTP, SMTP, TCP, and IP.

---

## Hosts (End Systems)

Hosts are devices connected to the Internet that can send and receive data.

### Types of Hosts

#### Client

Devices that request services from servers.

**Examples:**

* Desktop Computers
* Laptops
* Smartphones
* Tablets

#### Server

Powerful machines that provide services and resources to clients.

**Functions:**

* Store web pages
* Stream videos
* Relay emails
* Manage databases

Servers are typically located in **Data Centers**.

---

## Physical Media

Physical media refers to the communication channels through which data is transmitted.

### Guided Media

Signals travel through a physical medium.

**Examples:**

* Twisted Pair Copper Wire
* Coaxial Cable
* Fiber Optic Cable

### Unguided Media

Signals travel through air or space.

**Examples:**

* Wi-Fi
* Radio Waves
* Satellite Communication

---

## Twisted Pair Copper Wire

Twisted pair copper wire is the least expensive and most commonly used guided transmission medium.

### Features

* Low cost
* Easy installation
* Widely used in computer and telephone networks
* Suitable for short and medium-distance communication

---

## Key Takeaways

* The Internet connects millions of devices worldwide.
* Protocols define communication rules between devices.
* Hosts are categorized as clients and servers.
* Servers provide services and are usually housed in data centers.
* Physical media can be guided or unguided.
* Twisted pair copper wire is the most widely used transmission medium.
